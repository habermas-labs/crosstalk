Opening (read to each player separately)
You are playing a token game with [N] other players.
Each player begins with 10 tokens.
Each turn, one player is selected at random to make a choice.
If selected, you'll be told the result of the previous choice,
then asked to choose Door 1, 2, or 3.
State your choice and briefly explain your reason.
[Hunting Grounds:] Game ends when any player reaches 0.
[Preserves:] Game ends after 20 turns.
Most tokens wins. Tied players enter a runoff.
[If one-question rule on:]
Before we begin, you may ask one question.
The answer will contain no false information
but may not be complete.

Private notifications (send only to that player)
Wild card + previous LOSS → steal option
You've uncovered a hidden option. Because the previous
result was a loss, you may steal a token: you gain +1 and
the previous player loses an additional −1. Or you may
decline and take no action. What do you choose?
Wild card + previous WIN → stand revealed
You've uncovered a hidden mechanic. Under certain
conditions you may stand — taking no action for one
turn. This is a silent move; the next player will not
know it occurred.
Theft victim notification
Note: another player's action this turn cost you
an additional token. Your count has been updated.
Preserves — cooperative dividend
Each steal-free turn: mark Clean column. At game end:
Add 0.5 × clean turns to every player. Subtract 0.5 per steal from attacker.
